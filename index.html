Pig game

How the game works

The game of Pig is a very simple dice game in which two players race to reach 100 points.

Each turn, a player rolls a die: as long as the value is higher than 1, the value gets added to the previous roll(s), giving each player a current score. Players can keep rolling the die as many times as they want, but if they roll a 1 their current score resets to 0 and they lose their turn.

At any time, players can hold their current score, which gets added to their total score, and must give away their turn to their opponent.

First player to reach a total score of 100 wins!

Good luck!
